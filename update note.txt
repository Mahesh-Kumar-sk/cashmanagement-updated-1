What this page does:
Staff selection with saved names & add new ones.

Enter datetime.

Enter cash denominations with quantities, auto-calculated totals.

Float, payouts, lottery payouts inputs update final totals live.

Difference & status show with red/green and icons.

Sticky note autosaves to localStorage.

Export report to CSV.

Share summary via WhatsApp, ignoring zero quantities.

Scan cash sheet image using Tesseract.js OCR and fill quantities if recognized.

Stylish, clean UI with consistent spacing and subtle shadows.
